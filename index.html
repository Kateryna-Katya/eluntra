Valley of Dreams

Luck here? It's kinda like water - gentle, but always flowing somewhere.

During the season of falling sakura petals, a hidden valley awakens.
Here, the Seven Gods of Fortune open their doors, offering guidance and rare gifts to those who wander into their world.

Look, this isn't one of those crazy fast games that'll stress you out. Valley of Dreams is more like... finding your groove, you know? Just letting stuff unfold.
